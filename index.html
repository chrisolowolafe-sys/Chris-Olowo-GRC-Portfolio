// 01 — About

Background

Christian Olowolafe (Chris Olowo, PMP®) is a Cybersecurity Project Manager and GRC leader specialising in governance, risk, compliance, and emergency management programs — based in Calgary, Alberta.

With over five years of leadership across high-stakes environments, Chris has delivered security and compliance programs for public sector clients, environmental organisations, and healthcare systems — including emergency response coordination with Médecins Sans Frontières and a national cancer registry implementation for the Federal Ministry of Health, Nigeria.

His current role at H2Safety Services Inc. places him at the intersection of emergency management platform delivery and regulatory compliance — deploying the H2Command platform while building governance frameworks for critical infrastructure clients.

What sets Chris apart is the combination of real incident command certification (ICS 100–300), active PMP® credential, and a working GRC platform he built and maintains — not just theoretical knowledge.
