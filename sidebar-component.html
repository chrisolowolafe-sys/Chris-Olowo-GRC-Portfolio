Sidebar behaviour

Desktop: Click the ☰ button top-left to collapse the sidebar to icon-only mode (88px). Click again to expand to 280px. State is saved in localStorage and persists across page refreshes.

Mobile (<768px): The sidebar becomes an overlay drawer. Tap the toggle to open; tap the scrim or press Esc to close. Body scroll is locked while the drawer is open. Focus is trapped inside the drawer and restored to the toggle on close.

Accessibility features

The toggle has aria-expanded and aria-controls wired to the sidebar. The sidebar carries role="navigation" aria-label="Primary". All transitions are disabled when prefers-reduced-motion: reduce is set. A visible skip link appears on first Tab keypress.

Scrollbar

The custom wide scrollbar (14px, high-contrast track/thumb) is scoped exclusively to the .sidebar-scroller element via ::-webkit-scrollbar selectors. No global scrollbar styles are modified. Firefox uses scrollbar-color and scrollbar-width on the same element only.
